## 💡 Usage Tips

1. **Generate at 2× or 4× resolution** then downsample — keeps pixels sharp.
2. **Specify palette limits** for consistency: `"use only 16 colors from this palette: [list]"`.
3. **Ask for sprite sheets** when you need multiple frames: `"arrange 4 walk frames horizontally with 4-pixel gaps"`.
4. **Match shadow direction** across all sprites: lighting always from upper-left.
5. **Export as PNG with transparency**.

Once generated, drop them into `sprites/` next to the HTML and I can wire them in with `new Image()` loads replacing the canvas drawing code.
